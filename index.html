Kobe Bryant, Living Legend

Celebrating an Illustrous 20 Year Career

Kobe Bean Bryant retired from the NBA on April 13, 2016, ending a 20 year career of extended fury and dominance the likes of which we are not likely to see again. Over his long tour of the hardwood, he has compiled records upon records.

His career, by the numbers

  • 5x NBA Champion
  • 18x NBA All-Star
  • 11x All-NBA First Team
  • 2x NBA Scoring Champion
  • 33,643 Total Points (#3 All Time)
  • 81 Points in a single game (#2 All Time)
  • 12 3-pointers in a single game (Most All Time)
